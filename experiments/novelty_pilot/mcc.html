Stanford Language and Cognition Lab

In this experiment, you will learn words for new types of fruits from animals. Your task is to figure out which word goes with each fruit. The materials were designed for children. Even if they seem strange at times, we ask you to try to be as accurate as possible. You will be hearing sound, so please make sure your volume is turned on.

Meet the animals!

These little animals are hungry. They want the food that is on the tables. But, be careful, they are very picky! They only want specific food items and they will always name the food they want. You can give them the food by klicking on the plate. Before they ask for food, they go and get their plate. Watch out, sometimes a different animal returns. First, they will ask for food you already know the name for (apple or carrot). This will make you familiar with the procedure. Later, they will name new kinds of food and you have to find out which food they want.
